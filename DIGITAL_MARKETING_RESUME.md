=== OVERALL LAYOUT & STRUCTURE ===

RESUME LAYOUT OPTIONS:

Option 1: CLASSIC TWO-COLUMN
  • Left column (25% width): Skills, Tools, Education, Contact
  • Right column (75% width): Objective, Experience, Key Strengths
  • Best for: Clear hierarchy, ATS-friendly, professional look

Option 2: MODERN SINGLE COLUMN WITH ACCENT BAR
  • Full-width sections with colored left border or top accent bar
  • Consistent spacing and clear section breaks
  • Best for: Creative roles, visual impact, mobile-friendly

Option 3: HYBRID (RECOMMENDED FOR DIGITAL MARKETING ROLES)
  • Top: Name & contact (full-width, bold accent)
  • Middle: Two-column (Left: Skills/Tools; Right: Experience/Strengths)
  • Bottom: Education/Certifications (two-column)
  • Best for: Shows both professionalism and design aptitude

=== COLOR SCHEME ===

PROFESSIONAL & TRUSTWORTHY:
  Primary Color: Navy Blue (#1a365d or #2c3e50)
  Secondary Color: Teal/Turquoise (#0d9488 or #14b8a6)
  Accent Color: Gold/Orange (#f59e0b or #fb923c)
  Background: White (#ffffff) or very light gray (#f9fafb)
  Text: Dark Gray (#1f2937) or Black (#000000)

CREATIVITY & ENERGY (Suitable for digital marketing):
  Primary: Deep Purple (#4f46e5 or #6d28d9)
  Secondary: Coral (#f43f5e) or Coral-Pink (#ec4899)
  Accent: Yellow (#fbbf24) or Mint (#10b981)
  Background: White or very light purple tint

CLEAN & MINIMALIST:
  Primary: Charcoal (#374151)
  Secondary: Light Gray (#9ca3af)
  Accent: Bright Blue (#3b82f6) or Green (#10b981)
  Background: White
  Text: Dark Gray

TIP: Use color strategically - headers, section dividers, icons, and accent
lines. Avoid loud colors that distract from content.

=== TYPOGRAPHY ===

HEADING FONT (Name & Section Titles):
  Option A (Modern): Montserrat, Poppins, Open Sans (Bold, 18-24pt)
  Option B (Professional): Lato, Roboto (Bold, 18-22pt)
  Option C (Clean & Elegant): Playfair Display, Merriweather (for name only,
    sans-serif for rest)

BODY FONT (resume content):
  Lato, Roboto, Open Sans, Inter (Regular or Light, 10-12pt for body,
  13-14pt for job titles)

LINE SPACING: 1.2-1.5 minimum for readability

=== CONTENT PLACEMENT & MARGINS ===

MARGINS:
  • Top/Bottom: 0.5-0.75 inches
  • Left/Right: 0.75 inches minimum (ATS requirement)

SECTION SPACING:
  • Section headers: 18-24pt bold, extra space before (12-18pt)
  • Bullet points: indented 5-7mm from left margin
  • Consistent spacing between bullet points (single line space)

PAGE LAYOUT:
  • Header from top: 1.5 inches (reserve for name/contact/ATS header)
  • Footer from bottom: 0.75 inches
  • Use columns only below header to avoid ATS parsing issues

=== VISUAL ELEMENTS TO ADD ===

ICONS:
  • Email, phone, LinkedIn, website icons (thin line icons from Canva)
  • Skill icons: small logos for Google Ads, Analytics, Canva, Instagram,
    Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Place icons next to bullet points for visual interest (optional, ATS-safe)

ACCENT ELEMENTS:
  • Thin colored lines (2-4px) under section headers or as left borders
  • Subtle gradient accent bar at top (5-10px height)
  • Small geometric shapes (circles, squares) as bullet point alternatives
  • Light gray box backgrounds for key achievements (optional)

=== ATS-FRIENDLY REQUIREMENTS ===

CRITICAL DOs:
  ✓ Use standard section headings (Skills, Experience, Education)
  ✓ Include keywords from job description (SEO, SEM, social media, etc.)
  ✓ Use simple bullet points (standard circle or square)
  ✓ Save as PDF (not JPG/PNG) for best ATS compatibility
  ✓ Use standard fonts (no fancy display fonts for body text)
  ✓ Avoid text boxes, columns in header area, or background images
  ✓ Keep contact info in header (not in a text box)

CRITICAL DON'Ts:
  ✗ No tables or text boxes (ATS can't parse them)
  ✗ No headers/footers with important content (ATS may skip)
  ✗ No graphic elements in place of text (icons only as decoration)
  ✗ No two-column layouts for entire resume (use for skills section only)
  ✗ No background images or watermarks ( interfere with text recognition)
  ✗ No fancy borders that cut off text

3. CUSTOMIZATION STEPS:
   a) Change color scheme to your chosen palette

   c) Set up master headings (copy formatting for consistency)
   d) Replace placeholder text with your resume content
   e) Add icons from Canva's "Elements" > "Icons" (search "social media icon")
   f) Adjust spacing to fit one page (increase/decrease margins)
   g) Proofread thoroughly

4. EXPORT SETTINGS:
   • File type: PDF Standard (for printing) OR PDF Print (for digital)
   • Quality: High (300 DPI)
   • Include bleed marks: No (unless printing professionally)
   • Crop marks: No

================================================================================
                              QUICK DESIGN TIPS
================================================================================

1. WHITE SPACE: Leave at least 0.5 inch margins; don't crowd text
2. VISUAL HIERARCHY: Name largest, section headers prominent, body
   readable, subtle differentiation between job titles and companies
3. NUMBERS: Use percentages, dollar amounts, follower counts to quantify
   achievements
4. ACTION VERBS: Start bullet points with action words: Managed, Created,
   Grew, Increased, Developed, Executed, Optimized
5. READABILITY: Avoid long paragraphs - use 3-5 bullet points per role
6. CONSISTENCY: Same font for all body text, same bullet style throughout
7. PROOFREAD: Read aloud, use spellcheck, have someone else review
